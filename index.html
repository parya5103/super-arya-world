How to Play

Keyboard Controls:

  • Arrow Left/Right: Move
  • Space or Arrow Up: Jump
  • Z or X: Use Power-up
  • P: Pause Game
  • M: Mute/Unmute

Touch Controls:

Touch controls will appear on mobile devices

Game Objectives:

  • Collect coins for points
  • Defeat enemies by jumping on them
  • Find power-ups to gain abilities
  • Reach the end of each level
  • Beat the boss in the final level
